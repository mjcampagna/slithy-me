I went to the opening of Craig Wedren's "My 90s" photo exhibit Thursday night, at which he also performed a short set of songs.

The exhibit is of polaroids Craig shot using a Polaroid Spectra camera while touring, working in studios and on sets, and elsewhere back in the 1990s, many of which are multiple-exposures. Given the material on display, it seemed appropriate that I should shoot the evening in multiple exposures.

The images below were shot with the Fujifilm X-Pro2, either as in-camera multiple exposures, or using Film Simulation Bracketing (ACROS R, ACROS G, Pro Neg Hi) and merged using my technicolor processing technique. Additional treatment performed in Capture One 12.
